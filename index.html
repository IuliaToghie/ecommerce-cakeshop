I was once in Paris at a family restaurant, intimate and cozy. One night, enjoying a glass of red wine and looking upon Tour Eiffel, it hit me a memorizing thought of creating an intriguing, yet astonishing cake shop. It all started when I fisrt tasted a Parisian Macaroon made out of fresh strawberries, cloudy, but yet crunchy at the first bite. Our pastries are inspired by this Parisian air that I manage to pick up when tasting this divine Parisian Macaroon. Therefore, we invite you to try pure pastries and get inspire by the French air that dominates our store concept.
